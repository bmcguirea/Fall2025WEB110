About Me

I’m Brittany McGuire, a UX & Digital Marketing lead focused on subscriber onboarding, lifecycle campaigns, and web experience optimization. At Aviation Week Network (Informa), I design data driven journeys that help new readers activate quickly, discover value, and manage their accounts with ease. I love turning complex processes into clear steps whether that’s an Eloqua email flow, a responsive landing page, or a streamlined post login experience.

I’m currently completing my AAS in Marketing Management at JCCC and hold certifications in Digital Events (VEI), HubSpot Email Marketing, HubSpot Social Media Marketing, LinkedIn Content Strategy, LinkedIn Marketing Strategy, and LinkedIn Content and Creative Design. My work blends UX writing, analytics, and collaboration across product, analytics, and audience teams to deliver measurable impact.
